Time & Date

Timezone affects all event log timestamps. NTP keeps the Pi clock accurate automatically. Use manual entry only if NTP is unavailable or the clock is wrong.

Pi Zero 2W has no hardware RTC. On boot without internet the clock will be wrong until NTP syncs. Ensure the Pi has internet access at startup, or connect a DS3231 RTC module for offline accuracy.
